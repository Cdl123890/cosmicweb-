Welcome to the sky that never sleeps

Above us, the stars shine day and night, telling stories older than time.
Long before maps and clocks, humans looked up and used the sky to understand life,
seasons, and themselves.

This site explores how cultures around the world understood the stars. Learn the stories behind constellations like Orion, Cygnus, and Ursa Major, when they were discovered, and why they matter. Designed for beginners, this is a simple guide to the night sky.
